About Us

MetalTech Innovations, a proudly Canadian-owned and locally operated metal fabrication leader, has been serving western Canada with top-notch roll-formed steel roofing and siding solutions since 2020.

Our expansive facility, boasting cutting-edge CNC technology, empowers us to produce over 20 distinct product profiles, alongside a vast array of custom and standard flashing profiles

Driven by a steadfast commitment to timely delivery and efficient production, we've earned a reputation for excellence and reliability in our industry.

At MetalTech Innovations, sustainability is paramount. Our materials, derived from 90% recycled steel, not only offer superior performance but also reflect the vibrant colors, textures, and styles inspired by modern contemporary designs.
